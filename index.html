Breaking news: Class 10 Results to be Decided by Rock-Paper-Scissors Tournament!
By Staff Reporter
Wednesday, 18 March 2025

New Delhi – In an unprecedented move, the Education Board has announced that this year’s Class 10 results will be determined through an intense nationwide Rock-Paper-Scissors (RPS) tournament.

Officials claim this innovative method will make results more “exciting and unpredictable”—just like the exam papers.


The GAME Plan 🎮

Students will gather in school auditoriums, where they will battle their peers in best-of-three RPS rounds. Winners will advance until a national champion emerges, who will receive an automatic 100% across all subjects.

Students' Reactions

  • Rahul, a top performer, screamed, "Fourteen years of school for this?!"
  • Amit celebrated, saying, “I’m finally on an equal playing field!”
  • Priya, a self-proclaimed RPS expert, whispered, “It’s all about reading the opponent’s mind.”

The Aftermath

If successful, officials say they may introduce “Uno Reverse Card Grading” next year.
